My Story

I'm a Berlin-based Jr. UX Designer. To transform my career from finance into the tech field, I completed UX Bootcamp at Career Foundry in June 2020. During Bootcamp, I established a solid understanding of user-centered design by practicing every phase of it in an individual project.

Between June-August 2020, I involved in Frontend Development for Designers specialization course to have a basic understanding of web development so that I can better communicate with developers in terms of technological feasibility.

I have 7 years of experience in commercial lending as an underwriter and lending supervisor. My prior roles enabled me to gain invaluable transferable skills such as interviewing, researching, collaborating with other teams, negotiation, problem-solving, and managing stakeholders.

I'm currently volunteering at Philia, the women empowerment community, and looking forward to practicing my new tech skills in real-world projects and be a key team member thanks to my strong soft skills.
